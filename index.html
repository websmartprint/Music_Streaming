What is Fluss

Fluss is a free, open source music player that allows you to search, download, and play your favorite songs. Fluss aims to emulate the feel of popular music streaming services while still giving you full control of your own music. All music is stored locally and is available offline, no internet connection needed to enjoy your favorite playlists. To add music you can use the built-in search and download functionality, or add your own personal music files.

Features

Search & Download

Search any song you like and our built in search function will find and download your music from online.

Local Filesystem Playlists

Playlists are stored in local folders on your device. Add or remove tracks in the app, or for more control add your own music directly to the files themselves.

Playback Controls

Play, pause, stop, skip, loop, and shuffle. With more quality of life features to come

How to help

Find any bugs, typos, or mistakes? Any features missing? Report issues through github so that we can dilver the smoothest listening experince possible.
